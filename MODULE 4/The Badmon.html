Joey Bada$$ Fan Site!

Joey Bada$$ is a New York City rapper, actor and record producer. Originating from the Flatbush area of Brooklyn, he started rapping at the young age of eleven. He has since gone on to release two albums and several mixtapes.

While still in high school, Joey along with Capital Steez, CJ Fly and Powers Pleasent formed the Progressive Era (Pro Era). The group eventually grew to twenty plus members consisting of mostly rappers and producers.
